Nelson Mandela

  • Full name: Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela
  • Born: July 18, 1918
  • Nationality: South African
  • Known for: Ending apartheid
  1. 1918 — Born in Mvezo, Cape Province
  2. 1944 — Helped form ANC Youth League
  3. 1962 — Arrested and imprisoned
  4. 1990 — Released from prison
  5. 1994 — Elected President of South Africa
  6. 1999 — Retired from presidency
